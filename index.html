About

Hi, I’m Brody Chan—an engineer, musician, and creator. After earning my master’s degree in architectural engineering and working as an engineer for two years, I’ve decided to switch fields and pursue a degree in electrical engineering at Delft University of Technology. My interests lie in robotics, microchip design, and sustainable technology.

Beyond engineering, I’m also a classically trained pianist and composer. Here, I share my projects, research, and insights—whether it’s about electronics, music theory, or creative problem-solving.

Join me as I explore new ideas and push the boundaries between engineering and music. Feel free to explore my work and reach out!
